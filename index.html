Tahiti Papeete Mission

The Tahiti Papeete Mission is a humanitarian project by The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints located in French Polynesia. Dedicated missionaries from around the world serve with compassion, share their faith, and uplift the lives of the local people through acts of kindness and gospel teachings.

Find Out More

The Best Mission In The World!

Exotic Locale: Imagine serving as a missionary amidst turquoise lagoons, lush tropical forests, and pristine beaches. French Polynesia’s natural beauty is unparalleled, making it an awe-inspiring backdrop for spiritual work.

Papeete, the Vibrant Capital: The mission’s headquarters, Papeete, is a gracious city adorned with tall palms and abundant flowers. It lies on the northwest coast of Tahiti, one of the largest urban centers in the South Pacific. The bustling streets, colorful markets, and warm-hearted locals create an inviting atmosphere.

Diverse Population: Tahiti is home to about 69% of the country’s residents, making it the most densely populated island. The other 31% live on the 74 other inhabited islands. French Polynesia boasts a mix of cultures—Polynesian, European, and Chinese—which adds richness to the missionary experience. The Tahitian people are known for their welcoming attitude, and their strong love of family, fun, and God, make it a perfect place to proselyte.

Polynesian Cuisine: Food, or ma’a, holds a special place in local customs. Missionaries can savor flavors unique to the region, from ahima’a (food cooked in traditional earth ovens) to the adventurous fafaru (raw fish in fermented sea water). It’s a delightful way to connect with the people and their traditions.

About Me

My Journey

Born in Farmington, Utah, I never believed that I would have a connection with French Polynesia. This all changed when I received a mission call to serve in Tahiti upon finishing my High School education. I was able to spend about a year in this beautiful place, and will assuredly visit the islands for the rest of my life.

Passions and Hobbies

I am a big lover of the outdoors and any type of sport or extra-curricular activity. This matches well with all the amazing activities that can be done at Tahiti! I love the ocean, the mountains, and the chance to do activities with family and friends. One of my great joys while living in French Polynesia was playing soccer, basketball, and volleyball with the locals.

Life Lessons

Missions are amazing experiences that can teach invaluable skills. The people of Tahiti were able to show me what truly matters: family, God, heritage, and most of all love. I learned that material success does not equate happiness. While living and serving among this people I knew that they would do anything to serve me. This level of kindess is something I will strive to attain for the rest of my life.

Future Plans

After having this amazing experience, I vowed to keep Tahiti as an important aspect of my life. I am currently enrolled in an advanced university French course. I plan on earning my Global Business Certificate and hope to travel to French Polynesia as often as possible. My future will be forever affected by this miraculous place.
